# Reference Images Directory

This directory contains reference images used for e-commerce product photography generation.

## Directory Structure

### `/surfaces/` - Tabletop Surface References
Upload high-quality images of various tabletop surfaces:
- Wooden tables (oak, walnut, pine, reclaimed wood)
- Marble surfaces (white, black, veined)
- Concrete surfaces
- White backgrounds
- Textured surfaces (linen, fabric)
- Modern surfaces (glass, acrylic)

**Requirements:**
- High resolution (min 1920x1080)
- Good lighting
- Flat, even surface
- Minimal distractions

### `/angles/` - Product Angle References
Upload reference images showing desired product angles:
- 45-degree front view
- Top-down (flat lay)
- 3/4 view (classic product angle)
- Side profile
- Close-up detail shots
- Isometric views

**Requirements:**
- Clear angle perspective
- Professional photography
- Good composition
- Lighting reference

### `/products/` - Your Product Photos
Upload photos of your actual products:
- Clear, well-lit product images
- Transparent backgrounds (preferred)
- White backgrounds (acceptable)
- Multiple angles of same product
- Detail shots

**Requirements:**
- High resolution
- Sharp focus
- Good lighting
- Minimal shadows

### `/examples/` - Example Reference Images
Sample images provided to help you understand the expected quality and style.

## Usage

Once uploaded, these images will be available for:
1. **Surface selection** - Choose tabletop for product placement
2. **Angle matching** - AI will match the angle from reference
3. **Product integration** - Your product placed in scenes
4. **Context generation** - E-commerce ready images

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Consistency**: Use similar lighting across all reference images
2. **Quality**: Higher resolution = better results
3. **Variety**: Upload multiple options for each category
4. **Naming**: Use descriptive names (e.g., "oak-table-top.jpg", "45-degree-angle.jpg")
5. **Format**: JPG or PNG preferred

## E-Commerce Optimization

Images generated using these references will be optimized for:
- **Shopify** - Square and landscape ratios
- **Wix** - Flexible layouts
- **WooCommerce** - Standard product grids
- **BigCommerce** - High-resolution product pages

All generated images include:
- Professional lighting
- Clean composition
- Platform-appropriate sizing
- Print-ready quality
