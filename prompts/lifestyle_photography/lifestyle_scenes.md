## General Guidelines for All Lifestyle Shots

**Composition:**
- Table should be prominent but integrated naturally into scene
- Use rule of thirds for pleasing composition
- Props should feel authentic and not overly staged
- Depth of field with slight background blur

**Lighting:**
- Natural lighting wherever possible
- Avoid harsh shadows unless stylistically appropriate
- Create dimension and depth through lighting
- Match lighting to time of day specified in scene

**Color Palette:**
- Cohesive color story for each scene
- Complement the table's materials and finish
- Use color to reinforce mood and lifestyle
- Avoid oversaturation

**Authenticity:**
- Props should look used and natural, not brand new
- Create lived-in feeling
- Avoid perfect symmetry
- Include subtle imperfections for realism
