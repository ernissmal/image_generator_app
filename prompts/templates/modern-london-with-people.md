# Modern London - With 3 People

**Environment Type:** Urban Contemporary
**People:** 3 diverse individuals (mixed gender and ethnicity)
**Target Style:** Warm, social, lived-in yet sophisticated

## Template Structure

### PRODUCT FOCUS
- Transform this {{PRODUCT_TYPE}} image into a professional e-commerce photograph in a contemporary London apartment.
- Retain the exact {{PRODUCT_TYPE}} design, {{MATERIAL}} patterns, dimensions, and {{DISTINCTIVE_FEATURES}}.
- Alter only the background.
- The {{PRODUCT_TYPE}} should remain the hero product in sharp focus.

### SETTING
- Modern London loft with floor-to-ceiling windows and abundant natural daylight.
- Exposed brick or clean white walls, polished hardwood floors.
- Minimalist modern decor with designer pendant lights.
- The urban skyline should barely be visible through the windows.
- The {{PRODUCT_TYPE}} is centered with modern {{COMPLEMENTARY_FURNITURE}}.
- Warm, social, and lived-in yet sophisticated atmosphere.

### PEOPLE DETAILS
- Three diverse individuals of mixed gender and ethnicity.
- Dressed in smart-casual modern attire: sweaters, button-downs, and jeans.
- Naturally gathered around the {{PRODUCT_TYPE}}.
- Engaged in various activities: working on laptops, having conversations, or simply enjoying their coffee.
- The people should be slightly blurred or out of primary focus.
- Captures a relaxed and authentic, candid moment rather than a posed or camera-focused shot.
- NOT looking at the camera.

### TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Photorealistic quality
- Sharp focus on the {{PRODUCT_TYPE}}
- Slightly blurred people (out of primary focus)
- Natural shadows
- E-commerce ready
- 1:1 aspect ratio
- Neutral color palette with warm accents

## Variables to Replace

- `{{PRODUCT_TYPE}}` - The type of product (e.g., table, sofa, desk, cabinet)
- `{{MATERIAL}}` - The primary material (e.g., wood grain, fabric texture, metal finish)
- `{{DISTINCTIVE_FEATURES}}` - Unique characteristics (e.g., leg style, armrests, drawer handles)
- `{{COMPLEMENTARY_FURNITURE}}` - Matching furniture pieces (e.g., chairs, cushions, desk chairs)

## Key Characteristics

- People are secondary to the product
- Natural, candid interactions
- No direct eye contact with camera
- Smart-casual modern attire
- Diverse representation
- Creates lifestyle context while keeping product as hero
