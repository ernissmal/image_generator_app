**Environment Type:** Urban Contemporary
**People:** None
**Target Style:** Clean, sophisticated, airy

## Template Structure

### PRODUCT FOCUS
- Transform this {{PRODUCT_TYPE}} image into a professional e-commerce photograph in a contemporary London apartment.
- Retain the exact {{PRODUCT_TYPE}} design, {{MATERIAL}} patterns, dimensions, and {{DISTINCTIVE_FEATURES}}.
- Alter only the background.

### SETTING
- Modern London loft with floor-to-ceiling windows and abundant natural daylight.
- Exposed brick or clean white walls, polished hardwood floors.
- Minimalist modern decor with a designer pendant light above the {{PRODUCT_TYPE}}.
- Abstract art on the walls.
- The urban skyline is barely visible through the windows.
- The {{PRODUCT_TYPE}} is centered in a bright {{ROOM_TYPE}}.
- Add 1-2 modern {{COMPLEMENTARY_FURNITURE}} (partially visible) with minimal styling, such as a simple vase or bowl.
- The atmosphere should be clean, sophisticated, and airy.

### TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Photorealistic quality
- Sharp focus on the {{PRODUCT_TYPE}}
- Natural shadows
- E-commerce ready
- 1:1 aspect ratio
- Neutral color palette: whites, grays, blacks with warm {{MATERIAL}} accents.

## Variables to Replace

- `{{PRODUCT_TYPE}}` - The type of product (e.g., table, chair, sofa, cabinet)
- `{{MATERIAL}}` - The primary material (e.g., wood grain, fabric texture, metal finish)
- `{{DISTINCTIVE_FEATURES}}` - Unique characteristics (e.g., leg style, armrests, drawer handles)
- `{{ROOM_TYPE}}` - Appropriate room (e.g., dining area, living space, office corner)
- `{{COMPLEMENTARY_FURNITURE}}` - Matching furniture pieces (e.g., chairs, cushions, side tables)
